MVS  Mass Vigilance System

Info

In present situation, the information related to any malpractices or suspicious activities is given manually by the surrounding people to the respective authorities which requires more time.

Our proposed “Mass Vigilance System” mainly takes less time to inform to the respective authorities automatically. This project reduces the conflicts, suspicious activities and malpractices in the vigilance area when compared to the manual process.
